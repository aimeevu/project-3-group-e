VISUALIZATIONS

The interactive charts on this page brings the data together to compare the impact of different gun violence across the states. The visualizations on this page will also compare deaths by gun in Republican states versus Democratic states, as well as the difference in gun ownership across red and blue states.

Mass Shooting

The interactive charts on this page display the breakdown of gun violence injuries and deaths by state from Mass Shootings. Navigating through the drop down button to view the data by year will show the difference in gun violence activity from 2019-2022.

Interactive Map

This interactive map displays the percentage and number of guns owned by state with a color indicator to show the political affiliation of the state based on the 2020 election.
